**The Panic Pivot** is a data visualization dashboard that explores how periods of crypto market fear correlate with global AI job hiring trends.

The project is built as a lightweight analytical narrative: when financial uncertainty rises, professionals tend to pivot toward stable, future-oriented fields like Artificial Intelligence.

---

## Concept

Crypto markets are often an early indicator of broader market sentiment.  
This dashboard investigates a simple but powerful hypothesis:

> **When crypto fear increases, interest and hiring in AI roles increase.**

By combining:
- Crypto Fear & Greed Index data
- AI job posting distribution by location

The Panic Pivot highlights behavioral shifts during market stress.

## Dashboard Features

### 1. Fear & Greed Index (0‚Äì100)
- Visualizes crypto market sentiment over time

### 2. Escape Pressure Score
- A composite indicator representing:
  - Rising crypto market fear
  - Sustained AI hiring momentum
- Interpreted as **pressure to pivot careers**

### 3. AI Job Postings by Location
- Compares AI job demand across regions
- Includes Remote, United States, India, and major tech hubs

### 4. Key Insight Panel
- Summarizes observed behavioral trends
- Designed for quick narrative understanding
